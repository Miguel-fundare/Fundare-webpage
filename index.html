Grocery Retailer · United States
Selected Engagement (Illustrative Impact)
Based on real engagement · Details anonymised
Context

A regional grocery chain with strong top-line growth was experiencing systematic margin compression. Revenue was growing; profit was not. The commercial team lacked the tools to explain why — or where to intervene.

Diagnosis
  • ▸3 categories accounted for >80% of margin erosion — invisible without SKU-level P&L attribution
  • ▸40%+ of supplier-funded promotions were generating negative incremental ROI — confirmed via baseline vs. post-event analytics
  • ▸17 SKUs had identified pricing upside of 3–5% with low elasticity risk — previously untested due to lack of simulation tools
Intervention
  • ▸Deployed Revenue Analytics & KPI Systems (Fundare PNL Track) — giving full P&L visibility by category, supplier, and SKU within 4 weeks
  • ▸Activated Promotion & Trade Governance — restructured the promotional calendar, eliminating value-destroying events and negotiating improved supplier co-investment terms
  • ▸Applied Strategic Price & Pack Architecture — executed targeted price increases on 17 low-elasticity SKUs with full P&L simulation validation prior to rollout
How measured: margin impact calculated as delta between baseline P&L (pre-engagement) and post-event P&L at SKU level, controlled for volume and mix shifts. Promotional ROI measured via incremental volume methodology.

Promotional mix shifted toward value-additive events — higher incremental volume, lower cannibalization, stronger baseline protection.
Competitive Positioning
Supplier negotiations restructured around profitability data — stronger terms achieved across 12 strategic accounts without loss of commercial relationships.
