About Fundare

Built by practitioners.
Driven by results.

Fundare was built by RGM experts who spent decades inside retailers and FMCG companies — and saw firsthand how much margin was left on the table due to reactive pricing, untracked promotions, and fragmented data.

Our Mission

Enterprise-grade RGM methodology — without the enterprise price tag.

Most boutique and mid-market retailers and FMCG brands don't have the resources for a Big 4 consulting engagement. We built Fundare to give them the same analytical rigor, proven frameworks, and data-driven decisions — at a fraction of the cost.

Data Over Habit
Every recommendation we make is backed by your data. We don't rely on industry benchmarks or gut feelings — we build from your actual P&L, promo history, and market position.
Capability, Not Dependency
Our goal is to make ourselves unnecessary. We certify your team, build your processes, and deploy tools that run without us — so you build a sustainable RGM capability.
Speed to Value
90 days to first results. We're not here for 18-month transformation programs. We identify quick wins fast, prove ROI early, and build momentum from there.
